The Spider Awakens

Play as the veteran Edric Holmwoode as he burrows deep into the GREAT SPIDERS lair. Facing off against seemingly unstoppable odds, will you be able to regain Edric's honor and SLAY THE SPIDER?

Slay the Spider is a simple to pickup, hard to master game, blending turn-based gameplay similar to XCOM and Fire Emblem with real time trap dodging and exploration. Solve difficult puzzles, defeat powerful foes, and aquire new and powerful gear.

FEATURES:

Tile Based combat: maneuvering carefully, use your abilities to defeat the enemies that beset you. Make sure to manage your Action Points well, or else you may be in for a rude suprise. With deep gameplay elements such as opportunity attacks and buffs, there are infinite combinations of strategies that await you.

Real Time Gameplay: Outside of combat there are a multitude of things to do. Solve puzzles to earn new gear, dodge harrowing traps, or visit the local tavern and look for work. While you may master the turn based gameplay, you'll need to keep your reflexes sharp in Slay the Spider.

Old School Done New: While the images may make you believe the graphics for this game are strictly "old-school," players will be delighted to find a hybrid of modern animation principles mixed into a retro style. The end result leaves a nostalgic taste that still feels brand new.
